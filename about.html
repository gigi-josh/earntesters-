The EarnTesters Story

EarnTesters started with a simple frustration: too many agencies build beautiful websites that break the moment real users touch them. Fancy designs that confuse customers. "Perfect" apps that crash on actual devices.

We realized the missing piece was real human testing. Not QA teams in bubbles, but everyday people using products the way they naturally would.

So we built a studio that does both: we create stunning designs AND we test them with real people before they launch. That evolved into EarnTesters—a platform that pays regular people to do what they already do: use apps and share honest feedback.

Today, we're part creative agency, part testing community. Same mission. Same obsession with quality. We also help developers and businesses publish their apps on major platforms—available for sale at a price—so they can start generating revenue immediately.
